Renewable Energy Operated Paddy Thresher (MTech Project)

Overview: This award-winning project involved the structural optimization of a paddy threshing drum and the development of a solar-powered thresher tailored for marginal farmers in hilly regions. It was awarded the MTech Gold Medal and Best Poster Presentation at the 58th ISAE Annual Convention.

96.8% Threshing Efficiency
152 kg/h Throughput Capacity
450 W DC Solar Power
24% Cost Reduction

Structural Analysis & Innovation

Utilizing SolidWorks Finite Element Analysis (FEA), I evaluated the mechanical stresses on the threshing cylinder. A key innovation was replacing traditional mild steel bars with Glass Fibre Reinforced Polyester (GFRP), reducing the weight of the assembly while maintaining a safe operating stress of 57.29 MPa.

Solar PV System Integration

Technical Specs:
  • Solar Array: 2 x 150 Wp Panels (Series)
  • Battery Bank: 2 x 12V 26 Ah Batteries
  • Motor: 450 W DC Geared Motor (1:6.67 ratio)
  • Runtime: 5 Hours (Pure Solar) / 5 Hours (Battery Hybrid)
[Image: Solar PV System Layout]

Economic & Sustainable Impact

The developed thresher significantly reduces operational costs to ₹1,298 per tonne. Its zero-emission operation and portability make it an ideal solution for small-scale, remote agricultural applications where fuel access is limited and manual labor is high.
