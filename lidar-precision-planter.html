LIDAR-Based Precision Planter (PhD Project)

Overview: Design and development of LIDAR based tractor mounted automatic turmeric precision planter with a low cost mechatronic seed monitoring system.

This project focuses on enhancing planting accuracy in agriculture through advanced LIDAR sensors and mechatronics. It aims to provide a cost-effective solution for precision planting of turmeric, integrating robotics and programming for real-time seed monitoring.

Technologies Used: LIDAR Sensors, Mechatronics, Robotics in Agriculture, Python/R Programming.
