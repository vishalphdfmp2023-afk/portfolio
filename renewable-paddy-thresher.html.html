Renewable Paddy Thresher (MTech Project)

Overview: Structural Analysis of Paddy Threshing Drum and Development of Renewable Energy-Operated Paddy Thresher.

This project involved structural analysis to ensure durability and the integration of renewable energy sources for sustainable operation. It earned a Gold Medal and focused on improving efficiency in paddy threshing while reducing environmental impact.

Technologies Used: Structural Analysis, Renewable Energy Systems.
